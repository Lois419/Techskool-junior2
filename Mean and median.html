MEASURE OF CENTERAL TENDENCY (Mean and median)

Mean

The mean is calculated by adding all numbers in a set and dividing by the count of numbers.
The goal is to find a simpler, close value to the original number.
It is the average of numbers in a sequence
When we have a collection of numbers, we might want to find out the average number of numbers in it

How to solve for mean.

  • We count the number of numbers in the sequence
  • We count the total sum of numbers in the sequence
  • Next we divide the sumof numbers over the sum of numbers

    • How to solve for median.

  • Arrange the numbers in ascennding or descending order
  • Next, we select the middle number
  • That gives us a middle nuumber

    • Lets Take An Example..

    Find the median and mode of these numbers (2, 3, 7, 5,3)

  • First of all, lets find the mean i.e (2 + 3 + 7 + 5 + 3)/4
  • That gives us 5

  • Now for the median we first of all, arrange the numbers (2, 3, 3, 5, 7)
  • The median is 3!!
