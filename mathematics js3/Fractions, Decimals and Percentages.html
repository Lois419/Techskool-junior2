Decimal is simply another way to represent fractions, particularly those with denominators that are powers of 10 (e.g., 10, 100, 1000).

Converting from fractins to decimals and vice versa

  • Converting Fractions to Decimals: Divide the numerator by the denominator. e.g 2/5 =0.4( Its quite simple)
  • Converting Decimals to Fractions: Express the decimal as a fraction with a power of 10 as the denominator, then simplify (e.g., 0.75 = 75/100 = 3/4).

    • *Note that Recurring Decimals are decimals that have a repeating pattern of digits after the decimal point (e.g., 0.3333..., 1.272727...).
