L.C.M and H.C.F (Least Common Multiple and Highest Common Factor)

Introduction

L.C.M stands for Least Common Multiple and H.C.F stands for Highest Common Factor.
HCF is the greatest number that divides two or more numbers exactly. LCM is the smallest number that is exactly divisible by two or more numbers.
