Basic Operators( ADDITION AND SUBTRACTION)

Addition of Numbers

The easiest method of adding or subtracting numbers is by having the knowledge of place value system. By this system of arrangement, all units ( U), Tens (T), Hundreds (H), Thousands ( T), and so, are vertically arranged in line. As we stated earlier, numbers are written from right to left by their place values.

Examples.

if we were to add the numbers 1425. 1231 and 30,

  • we would, first of all add the unit numbers i.e 5+1+6
  • then add the tens of numbers i.e 2+3+3
  • next, we would add the hundreds numbers i.e 4+2+0
  • And finally, add the thousands of numbers i.e 1+1+0
    • Our total result would be : 2,686!

    Subtraction of Numbers

    Subtraction is the inverseprovcces of addition. In simple terms subtraction means taking away from another number.

    Lets take away 1,231 from 2,686

  • lets, first of all subtract the unit numbers i.e 6-1
  • then subtract the tens of numbers i.e 8-3
  • next, we would subtract the hundreds numbers i.e 6-2
  • And finally, subtract the thousands of numbers i.e 2-1
    • Our total result would be : 1455!
