NETIQUETTE

Introduction

"Netiquette" could be broken down into two terms "net" and "etiquette".

Etiquette can be defined learning and practicing good manners, which are essentially rules for polite and respectful behavior in various situations.

The net on the other hand also known as the internet is a giant, worldwide system of connected computers that allows people to share information and communicate with each other.

understanding how to interact positively and responsibly on the internet, just like they would in person

Why is Netiquette beneficial?

online Safety:

It helps us understand and protect themselves from online harm.

Positive online interactions:

Promoting kindness.empathy amd respect

Cyberbullying Prevention:

Avoiding and preventing onlineharrasment and bullying

Reputation Management:

Maintain a positive online reputation

Critical thinking:

Evaluating online information and making informed decisions.

Practical Netiquette tips...

Be polite and courteous:

Use "please" and "thank you," avoid offensive language, and be mindful of cultural differences.

Use proper grammar and spelling:

Avoid slang, abbreviations, and excessive use of exclamation points or all caps, which can be misinterpreted as yelling.

Fact-check before sharing:

Avoid spreading misinformation by verifying information before reposting or sharing.

S.A.F.E

  • Share Wisely
  • Act Kindly
  • Fact check information
  • Embrace respect online
