BASIC MANNERS

Manners

Good manners are simply about being polite, respectful, and considerate of others in your interactions and behavior. They involve showing kindness, using respectful language, and being mindful of how your actions affect those around you.

Why are good manners beneficial?

Show respect:

Good manners demonstrate that you value and appreciate others.

Build positive relationships:

Politeness and consideration foster trust and stronger connections with family, friends, and colleagues

Create a positive environment:

Good manners make social interactions more pleasant and enjoyable for everyone.

Reflect character:

Being well-mannered shows that you were raised with good values and have a positive outlook on life.

Lead to success:

Good manners can open doors to new opportunities and help you navigate social situations with confidence.

Good manners we could learn...

Saying "please" and "thank you":

This shows appreciation and respect.

Listening when others are talking

Showing respect and enhancing communication.

Apologizing when necessary:

Taking responsibility for mistakes and helping to mend relationships.
