Etiquette Course Page

Etiquestte can be the set of social skills and behaviors that help children interact politely and respectfully with others. It encompasses good manners, appropriate communication, and understanding social expectations in different situations. Teaching etiquette to children is important because it helps them build confidence, succeed in social settings, and show respect for others.
